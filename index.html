For those who are on the bench...

Welcome to our extraordinary world of freedom, liberty and opportunities for growth. You will have time to take care of yourself, plan the next steps and improve your skills and talents. Let's enjoy this huge opportunity to make a better version of yourself.

We love you!

We love people on the bench, as either they are available to get help or this is the good place to be. In a one team organization, a good bench means performance and potential. Never forget it, the bench is powerful! So what's next, let's see what to do on the bench.

It's time to read!

It's time to read to enrich your knowledge and your mental scope. Read to perform better, read for new ideas, read to realize new things and capitalize on experience. Read to learn new ways of working, new ways to think! Read is a demanding activity but it worth it!

It's time to train!

At the time of digitization and online courses available on companies and public platforms, there are no excuses to learn and train online. You are accountable for this, so let's use your precious time to get new skills, improve your potential and negociate salary increases.

Let's take care of you!

Take care of yourself, take time to identify what's important for you and in your life. Project to travel, to discover new things, practice music, video games, home staging, everything that makes your life better than ever. Strong foundations for an amazing professional life!

It's BBQ time!

If laziness defeats you, it's time to reach out to new cooking and charcoal experiences. Find, read and test new cooking techniques, take care of your family and arteries. Be you, be proud of you because you can be do what we want to do.

Six on the bench!

Enjoy bench time with your benchmates and spend time to recognize your skills and your progress in life, gratefully, and use this time wisely to take a step back and consider all opportunities and benefits you can get from the situation. It can be training, certifications, time to prepare a world tour, next career steps and so on.
Of course do not forget to drink a nice cocktail while considering all options, it's usually inspiring!

Make your own way!

If you are exhausted by your job and the atmosphere at work, a kind of fed up with mediocracy, mischief or kakistocracy why not consider to go on your own way! You should benefit of trainings, advice and support while having time to read and share a lot of stuff, building your strategy, your business plan and so on. Maybe it is time for you to fulfill your full potential!
If you enjoy travelling, digital nomadism can be an appealing aspect of the path.

Do not stay alone

During bench period, do not stay alone and keep contact with your friends, family and colleagues too. Staying alone is not good because it leads to rumination and depression. Also take a step back from work and be grateful of being in good health, enjoying family and friends and do not believe everything from your company.
You are not responsible for being on the bench!
You are not accountable for that too!
Stay connected to friends and keep a safe social life!
